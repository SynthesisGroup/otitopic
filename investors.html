Company Overview


OtiTopic, Inc., founded in 2012, is a late-stage pharmaceutical company, with a track record of success in product delivery and drug device development. Otitopic has changed the route of administration of a reference listed drug (RLD) and is on track to file a 505(b)(2) NDA for sale and marketing in the US.
