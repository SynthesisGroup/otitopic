Recovering hearts, Saving Lives


Otitopic, Inc., founded in 2012, is a late-stage pharmaceutical company, with a track record of success in product delivery and drug device development. Otitopic has changed the route of administration of a reference listed drug (RLD) and is on track to file a 505(b)(2) NDA for sale and marketing in the US.

The effects of Asprihale are a distinctly more prompt, potent, and predictable PD response than the current standard-of-care.

Otitopic has completed a dose range study (first-in-man study) and is preparing for a pivotal PK/PD study to establish bioequivalence. The data required for approval is expected to be obtained by 2021.

Our Mission
